ALIEN / NOSTROMO
USCSS Nostromo galley & mess hall — Alien (1979), Alien: Isolation (2014)
Nostromo Mess Hall — Galley Wall
PRIMARY REFERENCE — The target. White mugs on a rail, twin coffee makers, brown storage canisters above, white locker panels flanking both sides. Every product in the sourcing guide maps back to this image.
Nostromo Kitchen — BTS Set Photo
Original 1979 set. Kitchen galley viewed from mess table. White modular wall panels, counter surface, overhead storage. Note the locker grid on the far wall — the TRONES mapping.
Nostromo Galley Wall — Storage Panels
Close-up of the galley wall storage system. White modular panels with dark slot vents, stacked vertically. This is what the KLGO sideways locker bank is replicating.
Nostromo Galley — 3D Fan Art
3D reconstruction by emelnikov (ArtStation). Viewed through doorway — mugs on rail, white coffee maker, food supplies behind grid window, white locker panels. Excellent spatial reference for the 24" column layout.
Alien: Isolation — Sevastopol Galley
In-game screenshot. Kitchen counter with white storage units below, white locker panels above. The Isolation team recreated the Nostromo aesthetic with slightly more industrial grime. Note the amber lighting on the right wall.
Source
MOON (2009)
Sarang Station — Lunar Helium-3 mining facility, directed by Duncan Jones
Sarang Station — Workstation
Clean white panels, blue-lit viewport, integrated desk and display. The most "lived-in lunar hab" aesthetic in film. Closest visual match to the Captain Murphy's workspace intent.
Sarang Station — Corridor
Hexagonal doorway, white paneled walls, blue accent lighting. The geometric door frames and panel-to-panel transitions are key design language elements.
Sarang Station — Rec Room
Treadmill, white walls, overhead panels. Shows how a functional utility space reads as "habitat" rather than "spaceship" — the distinction Captain Murphy's is targeting.
Source
INTERSTELLAR (2014)
Endurance spacecraft interiors — directed by Christopher Nolan
Endurance — Interior Module
Modular white soft-pack storage covering every surface. NASA-accurate stowage approach — every wall is storage. The white-on-metal-frame look is the KUGGIS/TROFAST mapping at industrial scale.
Endurance — Storage Walls
Full corridor view. White soft-pack modules floor to ceiling with metal frame structure. The density of modular white containers is the aesthetic target for upper shelving.
Endurance — Behind the Scenes
Nolan on the Endurance set. Shows the physical construction — real metal grating floors, practical white storage cases, functional hatches. Built, not CGI. Same approach as Captain Murphy's.
Source
FOR ALL MANKIND
Jamestown & Happy Valley lunar bases — Apple TV+
Jamestown Base — Hab Interior
Storage lockers, strapped cargo bags, galley area visible through window. The most grounded, functional lunar hab in current media. White storage + metal frame + overhead fluorescents = the Captain Murphy's tonal target.
Happy Valley — Galley / Kitchen
Working kitchen in a lunar hab. Industrial appliances, overhead storage, display screens. The "someone actually cooks here" quality is what separates a hab from a set.
Happy Valley — Command Center
Panoramic set photo. White panels, integrated displays, overhead panel lighting, equipment racks. Reference for how Captain Murphy's workspace zone connects to the coffee bar zone.

CONCEPT ART / RENDERS
Fan art, 3D renders, and concept work — aspirational targets
Habitat Common Room — Concept Render
Lived-in hab module with curved seating, starfield viewport, board games, bonsai, personal photos, bookshelves. The "people actually live here" quality. Note the equipment panels on the left wall, hexagonal carpet pattern (nod to The Shining), and warm lighting mixed with clinical white. This is the aspirational endgame — a space that reads as both functional and personal.
